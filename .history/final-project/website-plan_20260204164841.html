Maggot Key To Animal Farming

Reason for the Topic

I chose this topic to help farmer understand how to cut down cost of production with the use of maggot, by providing appropriate information.

Purpose of The Site

The major purpose of this site is to improve animal production,by providing training, consultations, ensuring implementation for increase quantity and lower cost of meat.

Scenario

Animal management is being too expensive because of high cost of animal feed, a large amount of protein is needed to make it feed making it feed so expensive, which reflects in the cost of meat.

Color Scheme

At the header and main Green will be used and black will used at the footer.

Typography
